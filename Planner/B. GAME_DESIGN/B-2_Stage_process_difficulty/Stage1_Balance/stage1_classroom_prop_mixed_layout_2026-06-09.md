# Stage 1 Classroom Mixed Prop Layout

## Goal

Stage 1 should read as a classroom disrupted by the zombie outbreak, while preserving the central survival and dodging space.

## Layout Rule

- Keep the central play area clear.
- Place large visual props around the outer classroom zones.
- Use clusters rather than a neat classroom grid.
- Keep all placed props outside the central `12 units` safety/play zone rule already enforced by tests.

## Placed Prop Groups

- Northwest:
  - One upright desk pushed toward the wall.
  - One tilted chair nearby.

- Northeast:
  - One abandoned desk.
  - One overturned chair.

- Southwest:
  - One overturned desk.
  - One unconscious student near the outer classroom edge.

- Southeast:
  - One abandoned desk.
  - One unconscious student, rotated differently from the southwest student.

- West and east outer edges:
  - Additional desk/chair clutter to make the room feel less symmetrical.

## Gameplay Intent

- The props are visual only for now.
- They should not suggest a reliable collision barrier.
- Placement should support atmosphere without changing Stage 1 survival flow.
