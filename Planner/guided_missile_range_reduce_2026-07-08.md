# Guided Missile Range Reduction

Date: 2026-07-08

## Decision

- Reduce the 보조배터리 미사일 (`guidedMissile`) maximum flight/targeting range by one third.
- Runtime range: `22 -> 14.67`.
- Follow-up adjustment: halve the reduced range, `14.67 -> 7.34`.
- Keep damage, cooldown, explosion radius, unlock condition, and card appearance level unchanged.
