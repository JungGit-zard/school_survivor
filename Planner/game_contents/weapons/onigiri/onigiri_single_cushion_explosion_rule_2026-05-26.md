# Onigiri Single Cushion Explosion Rule - 2026-05-26

## Decision

- Level 1 onigiri has exactly 1 cushion.
- The first enemy contact consumes that cushion and retargets to the next enemy.
- The next enemy contact triggers the rice burst explosion.
- Base cooldown is 5 seconds.

## Gameplay Intent

- Onigiri should feel like a deliberate bouncing projectile, not a rapid multi-bounce chain.
- The explosion should be predictable: first hit redirects, second hit bursts.
- Bounce upgrades can still add more cushion contacts before the final explosion.
