# Title unlock-all-weapons cheat - 2026-06-12

## Decision

- Add a hidden title-screen cheat key sequence for development and balance checks.
- Cheat code: `unlockall`

## Behavior

- The player types `unlockall` while the title screen is mounted.
- All non-starter weapons are stored as unlocked.
- Starter weapons remain handled by the normal starter-unlock rule and are not written to unlock storage.
