# Zombie Spawn Smoke

Date: 2026-07-05

## Change

- Added `SpawnSmokeEffect` to the shared `Enemy` runtime path.
- Every spawned enemy now briefly shows a camera-facing smoke sprite at its spawn position.
- The effect lasts `420ms`, grows, rises slightly, and fades out.

## Note

- No spawn sound was added. Audio changes require Sound_Mini involvement by project policy.
