# Box Cutter Trail and Umbrella Scale Visual Note - 2026-05-26

## Visual Direction

- Box cutter trail should read as a sharp slashing/stabbing effect, not a blunt rectangle.
- The afterimage uses a wide triangular body with a pointed end so the attack direction is clearer.
- The visual is enlarged compared with the narrow hit lane, matching the readability style of the 30 cm ruler swing trail while preserving gameplay balance.
- Umbrella guard visual size is reduced to two thirds to avoid covering too much of the player area.

## Reference Basis

- Follow the existing cartoon/toon-style weapon language.
- The change affects weapon VFX only, not character or monster rendering style.
