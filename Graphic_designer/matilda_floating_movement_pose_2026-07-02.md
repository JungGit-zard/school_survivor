# Matilda Floating Movement Pose - 2026-07-02

## Direction

- Matilda should not read as standing on the ground.
- Idle pose: her body sits slightly above the ground and gently bobs up and down.
- Idle pose does not use the forward chase lean.
- Movement pose: the upper body and head push forward.
- Movement pose: feet trail behind the body to make the motion feel like hovering pursuit instead of walking.

## Constraints Kept

- 3D toon character.
- Existing chibi succubus silhouette.
- Existing face texture.
- Existing hair, wings, horns, ribbon, skirt, and boot identity.
