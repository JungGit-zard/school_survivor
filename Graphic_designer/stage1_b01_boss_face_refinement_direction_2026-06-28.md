# Stage 1 B01 Boss Face Refinement Direction

Date: 2026-06-28 18:59 KST

## Visual Direction

The Stage 1 boss face should match the blocky original reference more closely by reading as a simple low-poly zombie expression, not a busy cluster of small props.

## Applied Rules

- Keep the existing three.js toon rendering and outline style.
- Keep the green skin, dark hair, suit, red tie, and brown pants identity.
- Simplify the face into a few readable blocks:
  - damaged dark eye
  - light square eye
  - small dark mouth
  - minimal tooth highlight
- Avoid adding extra scars, gore, glow, or small decorative marks on the face.

## Result Target

At gameplay camera distance, the boss should read as "blocky suit zombie with a clear face" rather than a noisy face pattern.
