# Zombie Death Scatter Variant Direction - 2026-06-28

## Direction

- Strong zombie deaths should keep the existing fast shatter feel but avoid repeating the same fragment silhouette.
- Add three readable fragment patterns:
  - `ring`: pieces push outward as a broad circular pop.
  - `fountain`: pieces kick upward before falling, like a vertical burst.
  - `cross`: pieces snap outward along four strong directions.

## Constraint

- Keep the effect as three.js 3D toon-rendered body-part fragments.
- Do not replace the zombie with 2D particles or flat sprite effects.
