# Shark Missile Weapon Concept - 2026-06-09

## Purpose

Create the 2D concept direction for the planned `sharkMissile` weapon.

The weapon is intentionally much slower than `scienceFlask`, but each launch should feel heavier and more decisive. The in-game version should read as a playful classroom-science weapon rather than a realistic military missile.

## Visual Direction

- Shape: shark head and missile body combined into a toy-like science weapon.
- Silhouette: large shark nose, dorsal fin, side fins, and strong rear flame.
- Color: deep blue shark body, cream jaw, yellow hazard stripe, orange thrust flame, small cyan science capsule.
- Style: low-poly toon rendering with black outline, matching the current Roblox-like classroom prop direction.

## Runtime Translation

- Use simple three.js primitives: boxes, cones, and cylinders.
- Keep the shark head, teeth, dorsal fin, engine, and flame as separate readable parts.
- Avoid complex small panel details that disappear at the game camera distance.
- The final in-game implementation should prefer screen readability over dense modeling.
