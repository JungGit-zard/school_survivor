# Matilda forward lean visual direction - 2026-07-03

## Direction

- Matilda's straight movement pose leans the upper body forward by 45 degrees.
- The body center stays visually anchored; the lean pivots around the torso center instead of sliding the whole model forward.
- Legs and boots are parented to the moving body group so they remain attached to the tilted torso.
- Feet stay neutral relative to the body so the pose reads as a clean forward glide, not a running step.
- Toon 3D mesh, outline, face texture, wings, hair, and existing idle hover style remain unchanged.
