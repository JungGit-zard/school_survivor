# Umbrella Guard Five Color Palette - 2026-05-25

## Palette

| Color | HEX | Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cotton candy pink | `#FFC9DE` | Main canopy panels and handle accent |
| Baby blue | `#C9E4FF` | Secondary canopy panels |
| Lime | `#ACFF9F` | Accent rim panels and burst fragments |
| Mint cream | `#D4F4DD` | Soft support panel and ribs |
| Vanilla white | `#FFF6E5` | Highlight panel, rim highlights, explosion ring |

## Runtime Application

- The opened umbrella canopy alternates the five colors with cotton candy pink as the dominant read.
- Baby blue and mint cream keep the open umbrella soft and readable.
- Lime is limited to accent panels so it pops without overtaking the main pink.
- Vanilla white is used for highlights and the final explosion ring.
- 2026-05-25 update: the runtime model uses more vivid variants to satisfy the requested 50% saturation boost:
