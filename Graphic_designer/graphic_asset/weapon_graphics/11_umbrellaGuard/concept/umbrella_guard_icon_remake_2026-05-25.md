- Yellow umbrella canopy is the main read.
- Dark ribs divide the canopy panels.
- Blue electric shield ring echoes the icon's blue impact burst.

## Charge Warning Direction

- Red zombie charge warning should read as a directional warning, not only an area strip.
- The warning shape is narrowed and given a triangular arrow head so the player can read the charge direction before movement begins.
